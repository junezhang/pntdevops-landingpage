Red Hat Engineering Tools are a suite of services and applications for Research and Development to produce Red Hat products.

PnT DevOps is the team accountable for Engineering Tools.

To report issues or to request new accounts or services in Engineering Tools, create a ticket on the Engineering Operations queue by emailing eng-ops@redhat.com

Additionally, all known service outages for Engineering Tools are communicated via outage-list

Any other issues you can contact us by emailing pnt-devops@redhat.com
